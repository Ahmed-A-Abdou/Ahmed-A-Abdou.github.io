Unity Pathway Unit 1 Physics

Simple Driving Simulator

About this project
The first prototype in the Unity Junior Programmer Pathway. The project covers vehicle physics, camera follow, and basic player input — then extends into oncoming traffic, POV switching, and local multiplayer support.
What I Built
  • Character controller and camera follow scripts
  • Vehicle physics implementation with forward/backward and turning
  • Oncoming traffic spawning and management
  • Camera point of view switching between perspectives
  • Local multiplayer support
What I Learned
This was my introduction to Unity's physics system and how to create a responsive vehicle controller from scratch. I learned how to structure input handling cleanly and how camera follow scripts need to account for rotation lag to feel smooth rather than rigid.
