Balloon Cash Collector

About this project
The Challenge 3 project — applying the endless runner systems from Unit 3 to a completely different concept. Instead of a running character, the player controls a balloon floating through a side-scrolling world, collecting cash while dodging bombs. Same core architecture, entirely different feel.
What I Built
  • Balloon as the player with buoyant, floaty movement physics
  • Cash pickups and bomb hazards spawning simultaneously
  • Scrolling background adapted from the runner prototype
  • Cash collection plays a sound effect on contact
  • Bomb collision triggering game over
  • Particle effects on collection and explosion
  • Background music and full SFX — cash pickup, bomb collision, and BGM
What I Learned
The challenge was taking the same underlying architecture and adapting it to a movement model that feels completely different. A balloon doesn't move like a runner — it needs inertia, float, and a softer relationship with gravity. I learned how small physics parameter changes have a large impact on game feel, and how to build a spawner that handles two object types with different gameplay consequences cleanly.
