Simple Plane Control

About this project
The Challenge 1 project from Unit 1 of the Junior Programmer Pathway — applying the same controller concepts from the driving simulator to a flying vehicle with completely different physics behaviour.
What I Built
  • Flying vehicle controller with tilt-based movement and constant forward thrust
  • Camera follow system adapted for aerial perspective
  • Spinning propeller animation tied to speed
  • Camera POV switching between cockpit and chase views
What I Learned
Adapting ground vehicle logic to a flying vehicle taught me how different movement models require different physics approaches. The tilt-to-turn mechanic forced me to think about rotation axes more carefully than a simple car controller does. Reusing and modifying existing scripts rather than rewriting from scratch was also a good habit reinforced here.
